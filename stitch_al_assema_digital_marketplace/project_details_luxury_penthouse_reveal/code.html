Project Overview

The Apex Tower Renovation represents a milestone in sustainable urban development within the New Administrative Capital. Our goal was to transform a dated 1990s structure into a beacon of modern corporate efficiency and aesthetic elegance.

By stripping back the artificial partitions, we exposed the original architectural bones, integrating a high-end minimalist design language. The use of vast glass expanses and localized natural lighting significantly reduces the building's carbon footprint while creating an inviting atmosphere for high-net-worth tenants.
